About Solwave Enterprises (SOLWAVE):

Solwave Enterprises, also known as SOLWAVE, is a solar energy company based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, committed to building a brighter and greener future through clean energy solutions. We specialize in providing affordable, innovative, and sustainable solar power systems to rural and urban communities.

  • Solar home lighting systems
  • Solar pumps for farmers
  • Solar street lights and charging stations
  • Solar training and awareness for women and youth

At SOLWAVE, we believe that clean energy is not just a product — it is a path to empowerment. Our mission is to make solar energy accessible to every home, every village, and every individual — starting from Rajasthan to the rest of India.

“Our journey began with a dream — to bring light to every home and village still living in darkness.”
