🌞 Bindwa Khade Solar Project

  • Location: Bindwa Khade, Uttar Pradesh
  • Capacity:10 MW Solar Power Plant
  • Developer: SolWave Enterprises Pvt. Ltd.
  • Commissioned: June 2025


  • ✅ Highlights:
✔️ Monocrystalline high-efficiency panels
✔️ Integrated battery backup
✔️ 12,000+ tons CO₂ reduction/year
✔️ 100+ local jobs generated

The Bindwa Khade Solar Project commissioned in June 2025 is a benchmark in renewable energy, located in Bindwa Khade, Uttar Pradesh With a capacity of 10 MW, it leverages high-performance monocrystalline panels and battery storage systems to ensure consistent energy supply.

Binda Project

The Bindwa Khade Solar Project in Uttar Pradesh is one of Solwave Enterprises’ proud initiatives dedicated to empowering rural India with sustainable energy. Located in a region where energy access has been limited, this project is bringing a transformational change by harnessing the power of the sun.

With a focus on clean and affordable electricity, the Bindwa Khade project is designed to:

  • Provide uninterrupted power supply to households and agricultural operations
  • Reduce dependency on conventional energy sources
  • Promote environmental sustainability and carbon reduction
  • Create awareness about solar energy in rural communities

At Solwave, our mission is not just to install solar panels — it’s to brighten lives. This project reflects our deep commitment to making renewable energy accessible to even the most remote villages in India. We’re proud to lead the way towards a greener, self-reliant Uttar Pradesh.
